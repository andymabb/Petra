About Me

I started using homeopathy during my first pregnancy and with my newborn baby in 1998. Having experienced the gentle yet powerful changes in my child’s health when the consultant said no more could be done made me look into this form of medicine more and more.

I studied at the Welsh School of Homœopathy and received my Diploma and Licenciate in Homeopathy in 2005. I have been a registered member of the Society of Homeopaths since and work according to their Code of Ethics. I have full professional insurance and am committed to continuous professional development.

I run a face-to-face practice in Carmarthen in West Wales where I am based at the Welsh School of Homœopathy.

I have been teaching first-aid, self-help, introductory and advanced courses in homeopathy since 2004. I am available to hold talks and workshops on homeopathy.
